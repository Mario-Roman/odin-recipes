Molletes

Delicious Mexican molletes to enjoy whit the family

  • Bread roll's
  • Gouda Cheese
  • Smashed beans
  • Slice of ham (optional)

    • For the "pico de gallo"
  • Tomatoe
  • Onion
  • Coriander
  • lemon(optional)
    How to make the "pico de gallo"
  1. Cut in little cubes the Tomatoe and the Onion
  2. Also cut in little slices the coriander
  3. Mix it up in a bowl and add some Salt and lemon
  1. First you need to slice in two the Bread roll's
  2. Spread some smashed beans on the bread
  3. Put a piece of ham(optional) and put a slice of cheese in top of it
  4. Introduce it to the microwave for about 1 min or to oven at min temperature until the cheese melt
  5. Put your "pico de gallo" and enjoy!
