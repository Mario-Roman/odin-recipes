Sincronizadas

And easy and tasteful meal, Specially for the kids

  • Flour Tortilla
  • Slices of gouda cheese
  • Slices of ham
  1. Whit this all we need to do is start the stove and put a pan whit 2 tortilla's on it
  2. Put some cheese in one tortilla and the ham in the other one
  3. Now put the tortillas together and you have your sincronizada!
